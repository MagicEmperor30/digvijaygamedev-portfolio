Hi! I'm Digvijay — a game developer from Mumbai with a passion for building engaging, polished game experiences. I specialize in Unity with C#, covering everything from gameplay systems and enemy AI to custom shaders, Simple VFX, and mobile deployment.

Currently working at Furtados School of Music, where I'm building a suite of interactive music education games for learning the keyboard — shipped cross-platform to WebGL, Android, and Windows.

My previous internship at Dikshavision LLP gave me hands-on experience shipping Turbo Rage — a full 3D racing game — to the Google Play Store, complete with spline-based AI, Google Play Games SDK integration, and Simple VFX.

I love combining technical craftsmanship with visual creativity — whether it's a 2D pixel-art world, a low-poly 3D environment, or a procedurally generated endless runner.
